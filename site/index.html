01 — About

One ecosystem, every layer knowable

Kinetika is one person's computing ecosystem, built from the ground up. The point isn't minimalism — it's that nothing is hidden. Every layer can be opened, read, and replaced.

By principle

Materials show their age

Copper oxidizes, iron rusts — that's character, not failure. The palette, the wear, the seams are all on purpose. An honest system doesn't pretend to be new.

By practice

Nothing is hidden

The structure is the design. Configs live in the repo, services run where I can see them, and the homelab exposes itself rather than abstracting itself away.

By temperament

DIY, and built for agents

Dependency is a choice, not a requirement. And agents are first-class users here — the AX (Agent Experience) layer means machine-readable tokens and honest interfaces, for humans and machines alike.
